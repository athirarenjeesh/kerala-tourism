Houseboat in Alappuzha

Alleppey or Alappuzha fondly nicknamed Venice of the East is most often considered the Citadel of Houseboats in Kerala. With a labyrinth of inland canals, beaches, and lagoons, Allepey is the most often than not considered the starting or ending point to most Houseboat tours.

Nelliyampathy

Nelliyampathy is a hill station within the Nelliyampathy Forest Reserve, in the southwest Indian state of Kerala. The road to it, which goes through the dense forests of the Western Ghats, is known for its many hairpin turns. To the west is Kesavan Para, a vantage point with views of nearby tea plantations.
